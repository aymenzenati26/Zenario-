Our Leadership

Aymen Zenati Ziani

Founder & CEO

A visionary leader with a passion for technology and cinema, Aymen Zenati Ziani founded Zenario with the mission to create the ultimate streaming platform for movie enthusiasts. With a background in software engineering and digital media, Aymen has dedicated his career to building innovative solutions that enhance the entertainment experience.

Under his leadership, Zenario has grown from a simple idea to a comprehensive platform serving thousands of users worldwide, offering an extensive library of films and series across all genres.
